The Best Web Series According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Breaking Bad

*Breaking Bad* follows a chemistry teacher's descent into the criminal underworld as he transforms into a feared drug lord to provide for his family.

Money Heist

*Money Heist* is a thrilling Spanish series about a criminal mastermind who leads a group of misfits to execute meticulously planned heists on Spain's Royal Mint and Bank.

Wanda Vision

*WandaVision* blends classic sitcom styles with Marvel's cinematic universe, as Wanda and Vision live an idyllic suburban life that slowly unravels into a surreal mystery.
